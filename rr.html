Phantom

Entry to the International Autonomous Robot Racing Competition

Background

Every year our team competes at International Autonomous Robot Racing Competition LINK! in Waterloo. It's an exciting opportunity to work on robotics in a competitive environment.

My incredible team held down the fort and together we able to implement most of the planned features, including a Machine Learning based traffic light detection, a simulation of our robot and algorithm to remove shadows from camera images. I served as the software lead of the amazing team pictured below. I led the team remotely so unfortunately I could not physically attend the competition.
